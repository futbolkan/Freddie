Who We Are

Not just a club.
A way of life.

Satan's Order MC was forged in the streets of Los Santos by riders who refused to follow the rules of ordinary life. We live by our own code — iron and fire, loyalty above all, the road as our church.

If you bleed gasoline and breathe freedom, you already know who you are. The question is: are you ready to ride with us?
